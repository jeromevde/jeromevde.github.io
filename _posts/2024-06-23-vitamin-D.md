# What are the average levels ? And what should they be ?

Vitamin D is measured in *ng/ml* or in *mmol/l*. The conversion factor between the two is 2.5.

|ng/ml|mmol/l|
|-|-|
|10|25|
|20|50|
|30|75|
|40|100|
|50|125|
