# What is vitamin D and what does it do ?

Vitamin D has both an indirect effect on the body through increasing calcium absorption, and a direct effect as a hormone modulator on different cells with the right receptors.[Vitamin D and Bone](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3688475/) reviews the relative importance of the direct and indirect effect of vitamin D. In vitamin D resistant rats, a rescue diet fortified in Calcium seems to offset the negative effects of the unavailable vitamin D, highlighting the possible dominance of the indirect effect.
